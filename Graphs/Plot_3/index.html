Power transfer to load

The power suplied versus load resistance is:

$$ P_L = I^2R_L $$

where I is the current supplied to a load. This current can be calculated by Ohm's Law:

$$ I = \frac{V}{R_S+R_L} $$

where \( R_S \) is the internal resistance of the powersupply and \( R_L \) is the load resistance.
