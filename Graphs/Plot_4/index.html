Efficiency of a Power Screw

The efficiency, in percent, of a power screw, when the friction of the collar is ignored, is

$$ e = 100\frac{\cos \alpha - \mu \tan \lambda}{\cos \alpha - \mu \cot \lambda}\% $$

where \( \mu \) is the coefficient of friction, \( \lambda\) is the lead angle of the screw and \( \alpha \) is the thread angle.
